Rama is an incarnation of Vishnu, God of Protection.
Vishnu is one of a trinity of the three most important
Hindu gods Brahma the creator, Vishnu the protector, and Shiva the destroyer.
Vishnu has had nine incarnations
on earth as different beings.
One of these is as Rama.
Vishnu will have ten incarnations in all, the tenth is yet to happen !
